North Salt Lake City

My neighborhood is the wonderful city of north salt lake. More specifically the upper region up on the mountain. It is a beautiful place and I love it.

There is a lot to do on the mountain and in the city itlself. The eagleridge golf course is a 2 minute drive from my house and has some of the best golfing in utah.

North Salt Lake has incredible mountain biking as well. A lot of city made trails and community made trails make it a hidden gem for biking in Utah. These trails are also a couple minute drive from my house.

Food and Restaruants

There are a lot of great food options in North Salt Lake. Aside from the wide variety of chains, there are some of my favorite mom and pop diners here.

Nacho House

The best Mexican food in North Salt Lake!!! one of my favorite Restaruants ever and it is just down the road from the neighborhood.

Sun Shine Cafe

If you want the best breafast ever go here. It is sooooo good. Great memories, great atmosphere, just an awesome Joint if you ask me.
